Classic Yellow Cake with Chocolate Frosting

★★★★★ 1m reviews · ← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Buttermilk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Pillsbury chocolate frosting

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy, about 4 minutes. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each. Mix in vanilla.
  3. Alternate adding the flour mixture and buttermilk to the butter mixture, beginning and ending with flour. Divide batter evenly between pans.
  4. Bake for 28–32 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely in pans, then turn out onto a wire rack. Frost generously with Pillsbury chocolate frosting.
