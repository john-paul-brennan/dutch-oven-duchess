My Story

How It All Started

It all started in college at UMass Amherst. Between classes and late nights studying, I found myself drawn to the kitchen — experimenting with recipes, feeding friends, and falling in love with the process of making something from scratch.

What began as a hobby turned into a passion I couldn't shake. Now, living in South Boston and working as a research analyst by day, cooking is my creative outlet and my happy place. The Dutch oven is my tool of choice — there's something magical about low and slow cooking that just feels right.

This blog is my way of sharing that journey with you. Whether it's a cozy winter stew, a batch of sweet treats, or something in between — everything here is made with love and a good amount of trial and error.

The Food

What You'll Find Here

Dutch Oven Duchess is a recipe blog dedicated to honest, from-scratch cooking — the kind that fills your kitchen with good smells and your table with happy people. From hearty Dutch oven dinners to homemade sweet treats, every recipe here is tested, loved, and made to be shared.

Whether you're a seasoned home cook or just getting started, you'll find something here that inspires you to get into the kitchen and make something delicious.
