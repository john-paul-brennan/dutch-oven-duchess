Hi, I'm The Duchess

Welcome to Dutch Oven Duchess

I'm a research analyst based in South Boston, MA with a deep passion for cooking and baking — from hearty Dutch oven dinners to homemade sweet treats. I started cooking as a hobby in college at UMass Amherst and never really stopped.

This blog is my creative outlet and my happy place. Everything you'll find here is made with love, tested in my own kitchen, and meant to be shared with people you care about.
